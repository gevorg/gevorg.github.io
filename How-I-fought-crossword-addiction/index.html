How I fought crossword addiction

06 May 2016

It might sound funny, but I had an addiction to crossword solving few years ago. It started just from few scanword.ru contests and became a habit that was taking hours from me every day.

As a true developer who was familiar with java technologies I decided to fight that habit using my skills.

Program for solving crosswords

Yes, I created program that started solving crosswords for me and you know what? It started solving crosswords faster than I ever did. I called it flatback and published all sources on GitHub.
